#  Amazon Sales Power BI Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze Amazon sales performance through dynamic visualizations and business insights. This dashboard enables users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, evaluate regional performance, and make data-driven decisions using interactive filters and reports.

---

##  Project Overview

The Amazon Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance by combining multiple business metrics into a single interactive report. It helps stakeholders understand revenue trends, product performance, customer segmentation, and geographical distribution of sales.

The dashboard is built using Microsoft Power BI and includes interactive slicers, KPI cards, charts, and maps for effective business analysis.

---

##  Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Track yearly sales growth.
- Monitor profit and order metrics.
- Compare product category and sub-category performance.
- Evaluate sales across different regions.
- Analyze customer segments.
- Enable interactive data exploration using filters.

---

## Key Insights

- Office Supplies generated the highest sales among all categories.
- Sales showed consistent growth from 2011 to 2014.
- Central region recorded the highest overall sales.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Art and Binders were among the top-performing sub-categories.
